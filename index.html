The wall Addon

Detect and block ~17,000 Israeli-related websites and social accounts. Automatically and on all browsers

Just works, no action needed

All you have to do is Install the Addon. And anytime you open a website of an Israeli company you will see "the wall". They hold Palestinians behind walls, so let's put their economy behind our own walls!

Smart detection

Works for websites, LinkedIn company pages, Twitter and Facebook. More websites coming soon! Install it once and it will keep improving. You can easily dismiss the warning if needed.

Trusted data

The main source of the data is the latest Crunchbase list of companies with headquarters in Israel. Also companies with a founder or investor from Israel. More data like BDS list coming soon. The addon will show the reason

Works everywhere!

Works on Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Brave, Opera and very soon Safari. The best part? Also works on mobile browsers like Firefox mobile and Kiwi browser! Better detection and more data coming very soon.
